Mailing List
Collect and manage emails from the Join section on the site.
◆ Setup — Formspree (Free)

Formspree collects every email submitted on the site and stores it for you. Free for up to 50 emails/month. Follow these steps once:

1

Go to formspree.io and create a free account using your email.

2

Click New Form, name it VV Mailing List, and copy the form endpoint — it looks like https://formspree.io/f/xxxxxxxx.

3

Paste it below and click Save. The live site's Join form will automatically send emails there.

4

To view or export your email list, log into formspree.io and click your form — you'll see every submission and can download a CSV.

Settings
Manage your GitHub token and site connection.
◆ GitHub Connection

Token saved — you can publish changes to the live site.

To publish changes to the live site, you need a GitHub Personal Access Token. Follow these steps:

1

Go to github.com → click your profile picture → Settings.

2

Scroll to Developer settings (bottom of the left menu) → Personal access tokens → Tokens (classic).

3

Click Generate new token (classic), tick the repo checkbox, and click Generate token.

4

Copy the token and paste it below. Keep it safe — you won't see it again.

Help
Step-by-step answers for common tasks.
How do I upload a new gallery photo or video?+
  1. Go to the Gallery tab.
  2. Click the upload zone or drag your file onto it.
  3. The image appears in the grid. Click Edit Caption on hover to add a title and caption.
  4. Drag items to reorder them.
  5. Click Publish Gallery — the site updates within 30 seconds.
Keep image files under 3MB for fast loading. JPG and PNG work best.
How do I remove a gallery image?+
  1. Go to Gallery.
  2. Hover over the image — click Remove in the overlay.
  3. Click Publish Gallery to apply the change live.
Nothing deletes permanently until you publish. Refresh the page before publishing to undo any removals.
How do I add or edit an event?+
  1. Go to Events.
  2. Fill in Name, Date, Time, Venue, and optionally a Ticket Link.
  3. Upload a poster image.
  4. Click Add Event — it appears in the list below.
  5. To edit an existing event, click the Edit button on its row.
  6. Click Publish Events to go live.
How do I set up the mailing list?+
  1. Go to the Mailing List tab.
  2. Follow the 4-step Formspree setup guide.
  3. Paste your Formspree endpoint and click Save.
  4. All future email signups from the site will appear in your Formspree dashboard.
Formspree is free for up to 50 emails per month. You can export your list as a CSV anytime from their dashboard.
The site hasn't updated after I published — what's wrong?+
  1. The site deploys automatically — it normally takes 20–40 seconds.
  2. Do a hard refresh: hold Shift and click reload (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac, Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows).
  3. If still not updated after 2 minutes, check that your GitHub token is valid in Settings.
How do I change a password?+
