Our Philosophy

THE FREQUENCY

01

Blood Runs Velvet Gold

The feeling is as if blood runs through your veins as Velvet Gold — not only from our music, but from a deeper understanding of the brand and the collective as a whole.

02

The Power of Abundance

Abundance is the number one pillar of our success. Through music, art, fashion, events, dinners, radio, and collaborations, we will flow with abundance — creating something born entirely from love. Love is what the world needs, and it will be instilled into Velvet Vibrance at every level.

03

Intentional Inclusivity

The inclusivity of Velvet Vibrance will not look like what other brands offer. It will stand far apart — rooted in something more intentional, more authentic, and more rare. That vision will sharpen and deepen over time.

04

Drawing Artists Into the Wave

Through social platforms and collectives, we will draw artists of every kind into our wave. They will want to share their talents with us, and through that shared energy, we will all thrive — painters, graphic artists, musicians, physical artists, those who inspire flow, peace leaders, mind expanders, and above all, artists who spread love.

05

Why We Do This

God gave us one tool to share our inner frequencies: art and creation. It is an undeniable truth that love flows through those frequencies and reaches others — giving them the opportunity to see God the way we do. Acceptance, Growth, and Compassion are the fruits of this. That is the frequency. That is Velvet Vibrance.
